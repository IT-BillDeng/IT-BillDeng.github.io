We adapt Segment Anything with language prompts to segment epicardial adipose tissue (EAT) in cardiac MRI. Few-shot tuning plus text priors narrows the gap between large pretraining and scarce MRI labels, improving EAT boundary quality over vision-only baselines on cardiac MRI cohorts.
